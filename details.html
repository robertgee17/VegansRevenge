Vegan's Revenge

Vegan's Revenge is a fun combination of Real Time Strategy and Tower Defense games.

Play as a humble farmer, who command their animals to defend their farm against the evil vegan scientist's army of plants.

Gameplay

  • Breed your animals to expand your own army that can defend against the plants.
  • Slaughter your animals to gain more money to expand your farm's capabilities.
  • Destroy the scientist's army of plants to defeat him once and for all!

Controls

  • Left click - Select
  • Right click - Move
  • B - Open shop
